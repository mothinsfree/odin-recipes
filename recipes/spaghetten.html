Spaghetten with Sauce

Tasty Spaghetti with thick Tomatosauce!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatosauce
  • Tomatos
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook Spaghetti in Pot
  2. Cut Tomatos and Garlic
  3. Fry Garlic in Pot
  4. Add Tomatos and Tomatosauce to Garlic
  5. Serve!
